About Ssoluz

A place where you can

Ssoluz Cafe and Studio is more than a creative space — it's a sanctuary where artists, dreamers, content creators, and coffee lovers come together. Nestled in Taytay, Rizal, we crafted a space that honors both quiet contemplation and bold self-expression.

Whether you're shooting content, hosting a gathering, or simply savoring a well-made cup — Ssoluz is where your authentic story begins. We are proud to be Rizal's first ever self-shoot studio.
